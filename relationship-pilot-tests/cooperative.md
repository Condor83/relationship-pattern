## Edit 1 — Clarify when Resume Capsules are shown

A literal reader could show a full capsule at every checkpoint, increasing ceremony.

**Exact suggested edit:** In `SKILL.md`, section **Multi-session continuity**, replace:

> At every checkpoint and whenever the participant pauses, generate a **Resume Capsule** using `templates/session-resume.md`. Keep it concise enough to paste into a new conversation.
>
> - Default: show the capsule inline; do not store it elsewhere.

with:

> At every checkpoint and whenever the participant pauses, generate or update a **Resume Capsule** using `templates/session-resume.md`. Keep it concise enough to paste into a new conversation.
>
> - Default during continuous same-session interviewing: keep the capsule internal and show only the compact checkpoint.
> - Show the capsule inline when the participant pauses, changes sessions, asks for a handoff summary, or when you are about to lose continuity. Do not store it elsewhere unless the participant explicitly opts into durable storage.
