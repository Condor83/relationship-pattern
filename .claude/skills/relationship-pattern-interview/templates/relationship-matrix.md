## Evidence notes

For important claims, use:

- **Observed:** concrete event or reported behavior.
- **Meaning then:** what the participant believed at the time.
- **Meaning now:** current interpretation.
- **Alternative:** another plausible explanation.
- **Unknown:** what cannot be established.

## Transition prompts

- What from this case did you seek again?
- What did you decide to avoid?
- What belief about yourself, partners, or relationships changed?
- What behavior appeared in the next case?
- What important counterexample does this case provide?
